About 3 Nights and Done

It's free. It's non-profit. It makes learning fun for people of all ages.

You watch 1 hour of educational material each night, and in 3 nights' time it's done. Simple as that. You will get a sense of accomplishment instantly over say a weekend.

What can you learn in 3 nights? A lot! All our 3ND courses are practical questions that you want to know the answers for, ranging from how the Internet works to how hair grows on heads, from how to finance a startup to how to watch baseball games like a pro.

You will get:

  1. video lectures by the most articulate world-renowned experts who donated their time to this non-profit education charity, all in 10-15 minute modules
  2. instant feedback through a multiple choice question at the end of each module
  3. social learning through a discussion forum and wiki

If you can answer 80% or more of the multiple choice quiz questions, you will also receive an instructor-signed Certificate of Completion.

So, if you're wondering,

  • "How can we make online learning completion rate higher?"
  • "Why must online courses be based on university calendars in a semester-long format?"
  • "Why must knowledge be packaged in courses of weeks and months and start with a textbook?"
then join 3ND today and discover the joy of learning something fun, one weekend at a time.

Each 3ND is about the length of a long movie, but there's no charge for tickets or popcorn. Like movies, however, we release a couple of new courses each Thursday, so that you can pick that up right here for your Friday, Saturday, and Sunday evenings. Or any other day. We start with 4 courses on January 2013, and many more to come throughout its launching year.

So sit back, relax, and enjoy! Before you get to say "This is too much for me," you'll say, "I can do this!"
